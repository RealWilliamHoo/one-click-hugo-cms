倾听

清风徐来,

拂过树梢。

你在我身边,

却像从远方归来。

我们无言,

倾听律动的心跳。

两颗心坠入无垠的宁静,

树影婆娑,天穹无际。

Gentle breeze,

brushing treetops.

You by my side,

yet as if returned from afar.

Words take leave,

as we lend ear to pulsing heartbeats.

Two hearts steep in boundless tranquility,

slippery shadows, horizons without end.
